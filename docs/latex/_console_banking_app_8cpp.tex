This module store information regarding accounts such as account number, balance, etc. There are 2 kinds of accounts\+: Checking and Savings. Functionalities regarding withdrawal, deposit and interest for each kind of accounts are different. And they will be implemented in derived classes instead of \mbox{\hyperlink{class_account}{Account}} class.
